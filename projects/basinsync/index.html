Overview & Problem
Managing 3,600 projects manually

An Australian water utility managing a large infrastructure program across Sydney and broader New South Wales was running its project approval, vendor payment, and operational reporting workflows entirely through manual processes — email chains, spreadsheets, and disconnected applications.

The scale of the problem was significant: 3,600+ active infrastructure projects, 200+ external vendors submitting monthly progress claims against SAP WBS elements, and no unified platform to track compliance, approvals, or operational health in real time.

TCS was engaged to design and deliver a comprehensive enterprise platform — and I was the Technology Consultant responsible for functional design, team leadership, and end-to-end delivery across all modules.
