Overview & Problem
The procurement problem no one had solved

India's B2B market for Steel, Cement and Industrial Materials was fragmented, opaque, and deeply offline. Buyers relied on phone calls and spreadsheets to source materials; sellers had no scalable way to manage catalogs, pricing, or buyer relationships.

Mindsprint Technology engaged to build NirmanOne — a unified digital marketplace that would connect industrial buyers with verified sellers, automate the RFQ-to-Quote lifecycle, and give both sides real-time pricing visibility.

The challenge wasn't just technical. It required end-to-end functional ownership — from solution design and pre-sales to live delivery — across a complex Salesforce ecosystem with external API dependencies and multi-persona user journeys.

Key Deliverables
What was actually built

The NirmanOne platform comprised three interconnected modules, each addressing a distinct user persona and business need.

01
Salesforce Sales Cloud CoreAccounts, Opportunities, and standard Quotes — configured to support the B2B industrial procurement lifecycle with product catalog management and multi-level approval workflows.
02
Real-Time External Pricing APIReplaced manual price list management with a live pricing engine integrated via external APIs — ensuring buyers always see accurate, market-driven prices without seller intervention.
03
Experience Cloud Buyer MarketplaceEnd-to-end buyer journey: business registration, product discovery, RFQ submission, quote comparison, order confirmation, and payment history — all within a branded Salesforce Experience Cloud site.
04
Experience Cloud Seller PortalExternal sellers can manage their onboarding, product catalog, serviceable pin codes, and inventory through a self-service portal with role-based access control and API-level integrations.
05
Salesforce Console (Internal)Role-based internal console for operations and admin teams — covering deal management, seller verification, order tracking, and real-time reporting dashboards.
06
Opportunity-to-Quote AutomationEnd-to-end automation of the opportunity lifecycle — from lead qualification and product selection through quote generation, approval routing, and order confirmation — reducing sales cycle friction significantly.
