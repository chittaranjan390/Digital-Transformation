Overview & Problem
A distribution network running on manual processes

Rubber King Tyre Private Limited — one of India's leading tyre manufacturers — was operating its dealer network and B2B sales cycle through disconnected tools, manual follow-ups, and an underutilised Salesforce CRM. Leads were being lost in spreadsheets. Dealer relationships had no digital infrastructure. Response times lagged industry benchmarks.

The engagement required two parallel tracks: a strategic diagnosis of where the business was losing pipeline velocity, and the functional design of a B2B digital platform to fix it — covering enterprise buyers, dealers, and internal admin operations.

Rubber King Tyre Private Limited — a leading Indian tyre manufacturer with a national dealer network

My Role
Strategy & functional design

As the strategy intern, I was embedded with the sales and marketing team — responsible for diagnosing CRM inefficiencies, benchmarking against industry competitors, and designing the functional specification for a new B2B digital platform.

CRM Workflow Redesign
Audited existing Salesforce CRM workflows, identified bottlenecks in lead processing, and redesigned the end-to-end lead management process to reduce time-to-contact and improve conversion.
Competitive Intelligence
Conducted structured competitive analysis of lead response times and customer acquisition workflows across industry benchmarks — surfacing actionable insights that directly informed process changes.
B2B Platform Design
Designed functional specifications for a four-portal B2B platform covering enterprise buyers, dealer partners, and internal admin and sales operations teams — spanning 6 distinct user personas.
GTM Process Alignment
Collaborated with sales and marketing leadership to align proposed process improvements with customer acquisition goals — ensuring recommendations were commercially viable and operationally executable.
Salesforce Opportunity Automation
Designed the Opportunity-to-Quote workflow in Salesforce Sales Cloud — covering 6 role-based personas, automated pricing via real-time API, and configurable approval workflows across the sales hierarchy.
Dealer Management Design
Specified the dealer portal architecture — covering partner onboarding, commission tracking, territory-level inventory management, and performance dashboards for the regional sales team.
